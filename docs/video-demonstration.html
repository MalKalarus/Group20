Hello everyone,
We are members of Team 20 in the second-year “Introduction to Software Engineering” course in the Department of Computer Science at Western University, in London, Ontario, Canada.
We developed our project using javaScript, Grails, Groovy and React.
Our code was written in IntelliJ IDE. We used git for version control, GitHub to host the project and Gradle for build automation.
Our project, ResuME, is a web application designed to assist jobseekers in creating a resume and advertising it to employers.  Users post their name and qualifications to their profile.  The information in the profile could then be used to auto-populate a resume template.  
On completion, the resume could be analyzed using statistics and spell-checked with a grammar API.  Premium users would then have the option of contacting ResuMe personnel for further tips on improving their resume.
In addition, we also wanted to allow users to search third-party databases for job postings through ResuMe and save these postings to their ResuMe account. The user could also choose to make their profile and resumes public or private. Public resumes could be searched by employers, who could then save their favourite candidates to their employer user account for future reference.
On our welcome screen the user can learn more about ResuME by scrolling over the menu bar and selecting the ‘About Page’.  ‘Search Templates’ gives the user an overview of our available templates.  The user is also given an option to login if they have an existing account, or Sign up if they are new to resuME.
